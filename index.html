Montra Capital

An investment company formed by successful owners and investors focusing on small to medium hospitality businesses

Montra Capital is an investment company formed by a group of successful business owners and investors focused in the lower middle hospitality market. With substantial investment and operational experience, Montra Capital provides attractive exit opportunities for hospitality business owners by providing capital and management to ensure the continued success of the business we acquire.

Investment Focus

Montra Capital provides an attractive exit opportunity for small to medium hospitality business owners

Montra Capital is focused on profitable hospitality businesses in the lower middle market. Montra Capital is particularly well positioned to be an attractive exit option for retirement-ready business owners who are looking to transition from day-to-day operations. We look for opportunities that fit the following criteria:

Business Characteristics
  • Strong historical revenue growth
  • Well positioned in the hospitality industry
Financial Characteristics
  • Attractive margins
  • Stable and predictable cash flows
  • Low to moderate capital expenditure requirements
Situational Characteristics
  • Business owners seeking retirement or corporations seeking to divest
  • Experienced and motivated middle management planning to remain with the company

Benefits to Seller

Montra Capital offers liquidity to the owner and a sound transition opportunity to ensure ongoing success of the business

The Montra Capital management team and investors are successful hospitality operators and entrepreneurs who understand the concerns of privately held and family owned businesses. Montra Capital can help sellers achieve liquidity while ensuring the long-term success of their company. We seek to maintain the integrity, dedication, and reputation established by previous ownership.

Montra Capital is committed to the following:
  • Respecting strict confidentiality in the acquisition process
  • Incorporating the needs of the seller with regards to estate planning and tax issues
  • Focusing on long-term growth and not short term financial gain
  • Retaining personnel and providing new opportunities as the company grows
  • Maintaining and cultivating relationships with customers, employees, and the community
Montra Capital is a unique exit alternative:
  • Montra Capital will not look to consolidate operations or rationalize employees
  • Montra Capital maintains the unique ability to manage the company after an acquisition
